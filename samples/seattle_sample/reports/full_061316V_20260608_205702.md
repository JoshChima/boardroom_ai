## Purpose

The Council will consider Resolution 31669, which encourages landlords to adopt individualized tenant assessments when using criminal history as a screening criterion. The goal is to help housing providers align their practices with the Fair Housing Act's discriminatory effects standard, reducing the risk of Fair Housing Act violations.

---

## Agenda Item 3 — Resolution 31669

**Topic:** Best practices for the use of criminal history in the landlord tenant screening process.

The Council intends to discuss and vote on Resolution 31669. Members are asked to come prepared to consider the following:

- The HUD guidance on criminal history screening and what it requires of landlords seeking to demonstrate a substantial, legitimate, and nondiscriminatory interest
- The relationship between this resolution and the Seattle Fair Chance Employment Ordinance
